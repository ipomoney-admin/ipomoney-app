About Ipomoney.in

Ipomoney.in is a dedicated platform for IPO enthusiasts and investors. Our mission is to simplify IPO data and provide real-time updates on Grey Market Premiums (GMP), subscription status, and company fundamentals.

We aim to empower retail investors with clean, concise, and accurate information directly from official sources like RHP and SEBI filings.
